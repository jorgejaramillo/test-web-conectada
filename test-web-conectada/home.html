About Me

Behind the pixels

With over 7 years of experience in UI/UX design, I've helped startups and established companies create products that users love.

Based in San Francisco, I specialize in creating intuitive interfaces and scalable design systems. My approach combines user research, rapid prototyping, and iterative design to solve complex problems with elegant solutions.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring the city's coffee shops, attending design meetups, or contributing to open-source design projects.
